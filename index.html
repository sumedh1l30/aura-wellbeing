Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Aura Digital Wellbeing operates on a "Zero-Server" architecture.

1. Data Storage: All activity logs are stored in your browser's localStorage.
2. Tracking: We do not use cookies or third-party tracking scripts.
3. Transparency: You can use the 'Factory Reset' button at any time to permanently wipe your data from this device.
