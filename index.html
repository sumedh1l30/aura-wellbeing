Extended Privacy Policy & Data Sovereignty

1. Data Ownership: Aura Digital Wellbeing is built on the principle of User Sovereignty. Unlike traditional platforms, we do not store your information on any centralized cloud servers. Every byte of data—from app names to AI chat history—resides exclusively within your browser's Local Storage.

2. AI Confidentiality: Your interactions with the Aura Omni Assistant are processed client-side. The "Intelligence" is a neural simulation layer that does not transmit your personal feelings, goals, or queries to third-party advertisers.

3. Tracking Transparency: We do not use cookies, pixels, or finger-printing technologies. The dashboard only tracks what you manually input, giving you 100% control over your digital footprint.

4. Right to Erasure: At any moment, you can utilize the 'Factory Reset' feature in the System tab. This action performs a cryptographic wipe of the local database, leaving zero trace of your usage history.

5. Compliance: While Aura is a project build, it follows the spirit of GDPR and CCPA by ensuring that the "Right to be Forgotten" is built into the core logic of the application.
